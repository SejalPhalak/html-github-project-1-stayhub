🏡 Welcome to StayHub 🏡

StayHub is a user-friendly room rental platform that helps students, job seekers, and travelers find safe, clean, and affordable rooms easily. We aim to make the room booking process simple, quick, and transparent for everyone.

Our Mission 🎯

Our mission is to connect people who are looking for rooms with reliable house owners. We believe that everyone deserves a comfortable and budget-friendly place to stay, whether it’s for a short trip or long-term living.
